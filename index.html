01 · about

Background

Numbers tell a story before words do — that's what drew me into accounting and finance in the first place. I'm an Account Executive at LINK MIDDLE EAST LIMITED, where I make sure financial records stay accurate, reconciliations stay clean, and reports are ready before anyone has to ask twice. I started there as an Accounting Assistant in July 2024 and moved into the Account Executive role within nine months, a step that came from getting the fundamentals right: careful account reconciliation, on-schedule financial reporting, and catching discrepancies early. Alongside day-to-day work, I've built out my technical foundation through focused study in financial modeling, forecasting, and corporate financial statement analysis. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration and Management from Maharishi Markandeshwar University, and what draws me to this field is the problem-solving underneath the numbers — figuring out why an account doesn't balance, or how a set of financials tells the real story of a business.

→ Long-term goal: investment analysis and portfolio management.
